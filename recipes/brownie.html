Brownie

Description

Brownies are pastries with a dense texture and a chocolate flavour. Traditionally, brownies are served in small square-shaped pieces. Brownies often contain ingredients such as nuts or other ingredients that give them a special flavour to take the edge off the full chocolate taste.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • flour
  • cocoa powder
  • vanila extract
  • salt
  • choclate chips

Steps

  1. Prepare the Wet Ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter and sugar until smooth. Add eggs and beat them into the mixture until well combined.
  2. Add Dry Ingredients: Gradually add flour, cocoa powder, and salt to the wet mixture, stirring until just incorporated.
  3. Combine and Mix-ins: Stir in chocolate chips, chopped nuts, or any other desired mix-ins until evenly distributed in the batter.
  4. Prepare for Baking: Pour the brownie batter into a greased baking pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake: Bake in a preheated oven (typically 350°F / 175°C) for about 20-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with moist crumbs attached.
